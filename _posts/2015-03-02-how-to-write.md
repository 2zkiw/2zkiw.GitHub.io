- Inproper Integral:

a. If $\displaystyle{\int_{a}^{t} f(x) \,dx}$ exists for
every number t $\ge$ a, then

$$\int_{a}^{\infty} f(x) \,dx = \displaystyle{\lim_{t \to+\infty}\int_{a}^{t} f(x) \,dx}$$
