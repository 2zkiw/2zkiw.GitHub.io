- Continuity:

    A function f is continuous at a number a if:

        a. f(a) is defined(i.e. a is in the domain of f)

        b. $$\lim_{x\to + a}$$f(x) exists

        c. $$\lim_{x\to + a}$$f(x) = f(a)
