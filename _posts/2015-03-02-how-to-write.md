- Continuity:\n
  A function f is continuous at a number a if:\n
  a. f(a) is defined(i.e. a is in the domain of f)\n
  b. $$\lim_{x\to + a}$$f(x) exists\n
  c. $$\lim_{x\to + a}$$f(x) = f(a)\n
